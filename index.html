VISIT CHIHUAHUA WORKS IN FOUR SIMPLE STEPS.
1- SEARCH:

It's simple. On Visit Chihuahua homepage, enter the name of a monument you are looking for and click on search. Alternatively click on the search link on the top menu to view the list of all the monuments.

2- LOCATE:

Get to see where the monument is located by clicking on the monument information. It's even more exciting. You can get the directions from anywhere in the country.

3- RESERVE:

Reserve for a private tour for the monument of your choice. Click on the APPLY button to get the reservation form. We also follow up on the reservation to make sure you get a reservation on time.

4- TOUR:

Once your reservation gets approved you can now relax. Enjoy your visit to your selected monument in Chihuahua!
